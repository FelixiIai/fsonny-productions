Revolut — Around the World

Revolut Speculative

A speculative global spot for Revolut — the app that lets you spend, send and travel anywhere. We followed one traveller across the world in a single seamless thread: sunlit streets in Barcelona, the rush of Shibuya crossing, and everywhere in between.

Built entirely with AI, the film stitches together far-flung locations no traditional shoot could cover in one production — exploring dozens of cities and moments at a fraction of the time and budget, while keeping Revolut's bright, borderless energy.
