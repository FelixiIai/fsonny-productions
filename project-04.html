BookBeat — A Million Worlds

BookBeat Speculative

A speculative brand film for BookBeat — the audiobook app with over a million stories. The core idea: this case is about creating characters and building worlds, then weaving the brand right into the main story rather than bolting it on at the end.

One listener slips on a pair of headphones and is pulled through world after world — a cosy living room, a bustling kitchen, a pirate's deck, a 1920s noir back-alley — becoming a new character in each. Built entirely with AI, every persona, set and lighting mood was crafted as its own miniature world, all held together by a single object: the headphones that carry the BookBeat story through every scene.
